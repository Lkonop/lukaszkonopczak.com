Context: A witcher is sought to solve the death of a villager and a string of missing objects. On site we examine the corpse, its belly torn open. The witcher's trembling medallion points to magic at work. After a short search, we find a house spirit lurking nearby.

// NODE: NODE_SPIRIT_START
// ==========================================

PLAYER_01
[Anim: Crouches | Expression: Friendly]
Hey there, little one. What's your name?

SPIRIT_01
[Anim: Spreads his arms, tries to make himself bigger | Expression: Annoyed]
I'm not little at all! I'm very big already. I'm big Mieszko.

PLAYER_02
[Anim: Nods | Expression: Calm]
All right, big Mieszko – do you know anything about the death of the man from that hut over there?

SPIRIT_02
[Anim: Draws a round belly with his hands, laughs | Expression: Indifferent]
Maybe I do. So what? Am I not allowed to know? Because I, for one, know he was an awful thief. And a glutton. And he always wheezed so funny.

PLAYER_03
[Anim: Crosses arms over his chest | Expression: Suspicious]
A thief? Something tells me it was you doing the stealing.

SPIRIT_03
[Anim: Stamps his feet, clenches his fists | Expression: Furious]
No! Not true. He was the thief, not me! He started it! He kept eating my raspberries! I wanted him to stop, but he just kept eating them!

SPIRIT_04
[Anim: Shrugs | Expression: Indifferent]
So I borrowed a few things from the village too.

PLAYER_04
[Anim: Gestures with a hand | Expression: Thoughtful]
But he didn't stop stealing, did he? So you decided to punish him for it. What did you do with the raspberries?

SPIRIT_05
[Anim: Throws his hands up, laughs | Expression: Proud]
I enchanted them. When that glutton picked a whole basket, stuffed himself with them – then boom! The raspberries blew up in his little tummy.
