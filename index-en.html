About the Author

Łukasz Konopczak is a fantasy author fascinated by mythology and history. He writes epic, character-driven stories where gods and mortals are bound together in dramatic sagas about the consequences of choice, willpower and inexorable fate.

Alongside prose, he designs interactive narrative and quests for games – branching dialogue, combat and systems – bringing a novelist's eye for character and world-building to game writing.

Doom of the Olympus: Reborn

The gods are dead. For Nestor, that was only the beginning.

For centuries, the old Satyr trained the greatest heroes the world had ever known – Achilles, Jason, Herakles. His long punishment finally lifted, he expects nothing more than to die alone on the island that was once his prison. He has no idea how wrong he is.

„Doom of the Olympus: Reborn" is a dark reimagining of Greek myth, where mentors outlive their heroes, old wounds refuse to heal, and Chaos stirs once more beneath a restless sea.
